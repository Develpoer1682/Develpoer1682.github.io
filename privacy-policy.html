IF I BUY A PRODUCT BY MISTAKE, CAN I GET A REFUND AND RETURN IT?

While we do not offer refunds, we have introduced a policy allowing users to return products within 7 days of purchase. If you mistakenly made a purchase and did not intend to, please contact our support team. Note that once a product is bought, refunds will not be issued, especially if the code has been modified or used.

If you have modified the code and need assistance, please provide the following information when reaching out to us:

  • Your statement explaining the situation.
  • User ID.
  • Proof of purchase.
  • Date of purchase.
  • Reason for unintentional purchase.
  • Type of payment used.
  • Whether you have used or modified the product.
  • Any issues you are facing, including the product name and ID.

Before seeking assistance, please open a case with us. We may ask additional questions to better understand your situation. Thank you for your cooperation.
